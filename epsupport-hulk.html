Service History

Inducted into the fleet 2934 A.I.C.(After Interstellar Confinement), the Phoenix class Support Hulk is a 4th generation design. Primarily a support class vessel, this ship provides repairs, armament replenishment and advance construction when serving as an active part of a flotilla. When detached from an active flotilla, it's duties include strategic in-system construction and reclamation efforts. In addition to being one of the best support vessels currently in product, this class vessel also earned another distinction in recent months. One such vessel, the E.P. Sarratoga 2934-128431, was conducting a secret mining operations of a planetless star system close to I.E. (Immortal Empire) front lines. Part of a 1 destroyer, 1 frigate flotilla, the Sarratoga encountered a new ship I.E. ship of the line, now known to be a prototype Dreadnought class vessel. After a prolonged fight, in which the E.P. flotilla was forced to retreat, the Sarratoga was destroyed with all hands, allowing the badly damaged destroyer and frigate to jump safely via the gravity well. The last in system scans showed the prototype Dreadnought class vessel suffering power loss to it's primary reactor, after being rammed by the Sarratoga. While this engagement is classified, rumors persist throughout the fleet, causing an increase in commission request to Phoenix class vessels.
