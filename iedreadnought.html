Service History

Titan class Dreadnought is the peak of I.E. (Immortal Empire) technological and scientific engineering. There have been only 10 of these vessels constructed to date but the initial engagement results have surpassed the Emperors expectations. In the second recorded engagement by a Titan class Dreadnought, it destroyed a small flotilla of E.P. (Earth Protectorate) vessels including, 1 destroyer, 1 support-hulk and 1 prototype-frigate, while sustaining only minimum damage. This type of success has been recording almost universally, causing many requisitions to be placed by flotilla commanders seeking to add this formidable warship to their forces.
