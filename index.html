My Goal

As a graphic designer & web developer my goal is to integrate beautiful, clean and engaging designs into whatever media I'm using.

I strive for web pages and web apps that convey key concepts and delight users with simplicity, but which are also beautiful enough to win design awards. I also create presentations, business cards, creative ads, and most other designs with high attention to detail and a focus on both form and function.

My ideal job pushes me to excel in designing and implementing ground-breaking interactive and static media. I want a focused, transparent, supportive culture where I can learn from and help other smart employees as a member of a high-performing team.
